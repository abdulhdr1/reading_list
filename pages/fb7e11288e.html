The YouTube channel for UC Berkeley's Center for Responsible, Decentralized Intelligence (RDI), which researches the intersection of decentralization technologies and artificial intelligence. The channel features academic talks, research presentations, and panel discussions on topics like blockchain, AI safety, decentralized systems, and the governance of emerging technologies.

Berkeley RDI sits at a unique intersection: as AI becomes more centralized (controlled by a few large companies with massive compute budgets), decentralization technologies offer potential counterweights. The research explores questions like: how can we build AI systems that aren't controlled by a single entity? How do we govern AI development democratically? What role do blockchain and decentralized protocols play in AI safety?

The channel is a resource for anyone interested in the governance and political economy of AI ‚Äî going beyond the technical capabilities to ask who controls these systems and how they should be structured for public benefit.
